SAM
Simple
Bookkeeper

Sam is an intelligent AI bookkeeping assistant designed to streamline financial operations. Simply upload your bank statements in PDF format to a shared Prism AI Google Drive folder, and Sam will automatically extract and transcribe all transactions into a structured Google Sheets document.
Leveraging advanced AI, Sam suggests appropriate account categorizations for each transaction. Users can review and edit details such as date, transaction type, amount, description, and credited or debited accounts. Once approved, Sam seamlessly submits the finalized withdrawals directly to the QuickBooks API, ensuring accurate and efficient bookkeeping. Demo Video .

Meet Nicole
Your Restaurant’s
Smartest Team Member

Nicole is an AI voice assistant built to streamline restaurant operations and enhance customer service. Specializing in inventory management and customer interaction, Nicole provides real-time updates on fresh seafood market availability and can route calls directly to the appropriate team members. Nicole also automates reservations and order placements, saving valuable time for restaurant staff. Additionally, she’s trained in seafood preparation techniques and can guide customers step-by-step on how to cook their selected items, delivering both convenience and a personalized culinary experience. Jarvis is an all-in-one AI government contracting consultant, purpose-built to help business owners navigate and succeed in the public sector. From applying to government programs and securing contracts to interacting with government representatives and connecting with other professionals, Jarvis streamlines the entire process.
